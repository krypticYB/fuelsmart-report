Open Items & Resolutions
Active issues being worked on before month close
Requisition Approval Hierarchy In Progress

The Chairman requires a 3-level approval chain: the Supervisor forwards the requisition (without approving), it then escalates to the Manager, then to the Accountant, and finally to the Chairman for final sign-off. This is being configured in Zoho People's approval workflow settings.

✓ Resolution path: Mapping Department and Designation fields per approval level. Target: complete before April close.

Number Formatting in Reports In Progress

The manager prefers entering figures with both comma thousand-separators and decimal places (e.g., 1,234.56). Zoho's numeric input fields do not support comma separators at the point of entry — this is a platform-level constraint.

✓ Agreed workaround: enter clean numbers with decimals only (e.g., 1234.56). Zoho's reports layer applies display formatting automatically. Manager is being guided through this.

Attendance System — Hardware Dependency Blocked

Zoho People attendance tracking requires a physical biometric or attendance device installed at the office. The device has not yet been installed. This item cannot progress until the hardware is in place.

✓ No action on consultant side required. Will resume once hardware is installed.
